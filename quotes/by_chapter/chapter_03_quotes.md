## Overview

This chapter explores the psychological and archetypal dimensions of human pair bonding through Carl Jung's analytical psychology framework. Key themes include:

- **Collective Unconscious:** Universal psychic patterns underlying mate selection
- **Anima and Animus:** Contrasexual archetypes projected onto partners
- **Individuation:** Psychological development through relationship
- **Shadow Work:** Integration of rejected aspects in partnership
- **Archetypal Patterns:** Universal symbolic dimensions of love and bonding
